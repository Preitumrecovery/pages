Our Mission and Vision

Putting a smile back on our clients’ faces

Helping victims recover their crypto assets—without paying upfront. Many victims lose their life savings and even accumulate debt before realizing they were scammed. Because of this, Peritum Recovery only charges after assets are successfully recovered.

Technology

Launched in 2017, Peritum Recovery was founded by two highly skilled professionals from different fields: a security researcher and a retired financial investigator. Working together, they help victims of cryptocurrency scams recover lost assets, achieving a 97% success rate.

Our proprietary software is purpose-built to investigate, track, and recover lost crypto assets and regain access to frozen or locked wallets and hard drives.

It took two years to develop this software, first tested in early 2017 with immediate success. Since then, it has helped hundreds of victims, with over $24M in assets recovered.
